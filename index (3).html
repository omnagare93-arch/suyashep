Chapter 1: Foundations

The Evolution of Education

Education has constantly adapted, from ancient oral traditions to modern digital classrooms. Early education focused on rote memorization, while the industrial age brought standardized testing and mass schooling.

The digital revolution introduced online learning, but Artificial Intelligence promises a new and fundamental paradigm shift, moving beyond the traditional one-size-fits-all model.
